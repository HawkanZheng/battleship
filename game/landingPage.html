Battleship Rules

  • Player must first setup their board by entering the span of their ships.

  • Players take turns firing shots (by entering grid coordinates) to attempt to hit enemy ships.

  • On your turn, enter the letter and number that identifies a row and column on the enemy grid grid.

  • Hits are marked in red and misses are marked in blue. A ship is sunk when each unit of the ship is hit.

  • Player wins when all 5 enemy ships are sunk.
